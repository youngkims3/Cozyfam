The Big Apple
back

Embarking on a new chapter of my life I found myself trading the familiar streets of Korea for the bustling and hustling rhythm of New York as I pursued my ambition. The cultural shifts were palpable, and every corner of a neighborhood seemed to hold a new adventure. The towering skyscrapers and diverse faces formed a vivid tapestry that painted a stark contrast to my accustomed surroundings. Navigating my academics and adjusting to the dynamic pace of life, I felt a sense of exhilaration and, at times, overwhelming excitement. The kaleidoscope of cultures and the fast-paced lifestyle pushed me beyond my comfort zone, yet I embraced the change with an open heart. As I adapted to a different educational system and forged connections with peers from around the globe, I discovered the transformative power of embracing diversity, fostering personal growth, and weaving my unique narrative into the vibrant fabric of my New York college experience.
